# 🩺 Diabetes Risk Analysis & Healthcare Analytics Dashboard

## 📖 Project Overview

This end-to-end healthcare analytics project analyzes patient demographics, lifestyle factors, and clinical measurements to identify key factors associated with diabetes risk.

The project combines SQL, Python, and Power BI to transform raw healthcare data into interactive dashboards and actionable insights that support early risk identification and data-driven healthcare decisions.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Diabetes is one of the fastest-growing chronic diseases worldwide. Early identification of high-risk patients can help healthcare providers improve preventive care, optimize clinical resources, and reduce long-term treatment costs.

This project aims to analyze patient health records to uncover patterns influencing diabetes risk and build interactive dashboards for healthcare decision-making.

# 💡 Key Insights

- Diabetes prevalence increases significantly among older age groups.
- Obesity is associated with substantially higher diabetes rates than healthy BMI ranges.
- Patients with diabetic HbA1c levels have the highest likelihood of diabetes.
- Critical blood glucose levels are strongly associated with diabetes prevalence.
- Hypertension is associated with a higher diabetes rate compared with patients without hypertension.
- Risk segmentation enables healthcare providers to identify patients requiring early intervention.
